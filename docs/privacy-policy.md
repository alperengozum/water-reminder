# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-28

Daily Water Tracker (the "App") is designed to help you track your hydration. This policy explains how the App handles data.

## Summary
- The App stores your hydration logs locally on your device.
- Your hydration data stays on your device until you delete it.
- The App uses Google AdMob to show ads.
- The App does not sell your personal data.

## Data We Collect
The App stores your hydration entries (amounts and timestamps) locally on your device using on-device storage. You can remove entries at any time within the App.

## Data Retention
Hydration entries are retained on your device until you delete them in the App or remove the App from your device.

## Data Deletion
You can delete your hydration entries at any time within the App. Uninstalling the App also removes locally stored hydration data from your device.

When ads are shown, Google AdMob may collect data to serve and measure ads, such as:
- Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
- IP address and approximate location (derived from IP)
- App interaction and ad performance data

This data is collected by Google as a third-party advertising provider and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

## Third-Party Services
The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect and process data as described above.

## Children
The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information.

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL.
